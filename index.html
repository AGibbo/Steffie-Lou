ABOUT US

Welcome to Steffie Lou's Event Hire

We have beautiful Sweet carts and table top buffets to hire for all occasions. We cater for all events, weddings, corporate events, special birthdays, christenings and baby showers offering a variety of packages to suit your needs.

We can even personalise the cart to match your themes and colour schemes and we have other items available to hire including 4ft "LOVE" letters and post boxes.

If you're looking for something to add a special touch to your event, why not get in touch?
